My name is Henry Schiller.

I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in the Philosophy Department at King's College London.

henry.schiller [at] kcl.ac.uk

Before coming to KCL I had postdocs at the University of Vienna, the University of Sheffield, and Washington University in St. Louis. I did my Ph.D. at the University of Texas at Austin with Josh Dever and Mark Sainsbury. I am the Speech Acts area editor for PhilPapers.
